04 — FAQ

Questions you probably have.

What is Dots?
Dots is an open edition of generative tokens. Every mint is an 8×10 grid of 80 coloured MegaETH logomarks at a unique seed.
How does merging work?
Merge two dots of the same level to burn one and halve the dots on the other. The ladder runs 80 → 40 → 20 → 10 → 5 → 4 → 1.
Where is the art stored?
Fully onchain. tokenURI returns a base64-encoded JSON document containing a base64-encoded SVG. No IPFS, no HTTP, no mutable metadata. The contract renders the full image on demand for every view call.
Do colors change when I merge?
The seed stays stable. Colours are derived from the seed plus the merges history, so a merged dot inherits traces of every ancestor. The palette itself is 80 hand-picked hex values sorted as a smooth pink-through-purple gradient.
What is the Mega Dot?
The terminal state at level 7. Reached only by burning 64 single dots in one call via infinity(). Rendered as a single white MegaETH logomark on a black disc, on a white canvas — the one inverted state in the collection.
What gas does merge cost?
A single merge runs around 120k gas — two storage reads, one storage write, one burn. A full 80 → 1 ladder across 63 merges is well within a single testnet transaction budget.
